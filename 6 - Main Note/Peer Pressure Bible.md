# Peer Pressure Bible

## **Bible Study: "Standing Strong Under Peer Pressure"**

### **Key Verse**

Romans 12:2 – _“Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind.”_

---

### **1. Icebreaker (5 minutes)**

- **Activity:** _"Would You Rather?"_
    
    - Ask fun, slightly challenging questions like:
        
        - _“Would you rather always follow the crowd or always stand alone?”_
            
        - _“Would you rather be liked by everyone or respected by a few?”_
            
    - Discuss briefly: _"Why do we sometimes feel pressure to fit in?"_
        

---

### **2. Bible Exploration (10 minutes)**

#### **A. Understanding Peer Pressure**

- Read Proverbs 1:10 – _“My son, if sinful men entice you, do not give in to them.”_
    
- Discussion: _"What are common types of peer pressure at school or online?"_
    

#### **B. Biblical Example**

- **Story:** Daniel 3 (Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego and the fiery furnace)
    
- Summary: They refused to bow to the king’s idol even when everyone else did.
    
- Discussion:
    
    - _"What gave them the courage to stand firm?"_
        
    - _"What might have happened if they gave in?"_
        

---

### **3. Group Activity (5–6 minutes)**

**"Pressure Situations" Role-Play**

- Split into small groups.
    
- Give scenarios (e.g., _friends pressuring you to cheat on a test, skip church, join in gossip_).
    
- Groups discuss and role-play how to respond as a Christian.
    
- Debrief: _"What makes saying ‘no’ easier? What makes it harder?"_
    

---

### **4. Application (5 minutes)**

- Discuss:
    
    - _"How can we prepare ourselves before facing peer pressure?"_
        
    - Suggested strategies:
        
        - Remember who you are in Christ (1 Peter 2:9).
> [!bible] [1 Peter 2:9 - NKJV](https://bolls.life/NKJV/60/2/)
> 9. But you <i>are</i> a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that you may proclaim the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvelous light;


        - 
            
        - Choose friends who encourage your faith.
            
        - Pray for courage daily.
            
- Challenge: _"This week, stand up for what’s right in one small way and share how it went next time we meet."_
