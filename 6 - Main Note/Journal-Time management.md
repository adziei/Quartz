Time management is a skill used by many. Many different student will have different priorities when it comes to time management and so on, I myself have my own way of organizing my academic life and school life that I will share with you today.

I have tried to use multiple different tools for time management and study such as, Toggl, Forest, prosperity, and many more, but recently I have found a way that I am adapting to right now throughout this term, and it is too make sure that I have something assigned for each period at school, I also am trying to implement a system where when I get home I share what work I accomplished with my dad. When I work like this I find that some days I get home and my dad looks intrigued at my work or will also help me recognize that I could have done this work faster if i approached it in a different direction. I also have implemented an hour counter to my tech that shows just how much time I have in a day or week to actually do work and it sometimes makes me realizes that either I am up to date, slacking off or even high achieving. 

I Believe that if I add enough self reflection into my work I will be able to see just how much time is being wasted, in the end goal I will be able to see whether I am really interested into a specific project or not, such as a few of my past projects. when it comes to lost time I believe that my  biggest issue would be on my math work, where I have struggled to keep up with the rest of the class which love to go above and beyond since their future will be in the engineering field or the electrician, but I believe that I need to associate some special time just a little to get started at home to do work that I do not enjoy but that I do benefit form (Math , Journal, Reading (fiction))
